Fettuccini Carbonara

Description

Fettuccini Carbonara is a classic dish hailing from Italy containing generous amounts of cheese, bacon, and other delectable ingredients all gathered together into a truly divine form.

This recipe should take around 35 minutes to complete, including prep.

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 5 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 shallots, diced
  • 1 pound bacon, cut into strips
  • 1 larg onion, cut into thin strips
  • 1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine pasta
  • 3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 egg yolks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large heavy saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Saute shallots until softened.
  3. Stir in bacon and onion; cook and stir until bacon is evenly browned.
  4. Stir in garlic when bacon is about half done, once mixed remove from heat.
  5. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente. Then drain most of the water, but save a few spoonfuls.
  6. Whisk Parmesan, cream, and egg yolks together in medium bowl to form a cream mixture.
  7. Add still hot pasta water to cream and mix.
  8. Pour bacon mix over pasta; stir in cream mixture.
  9. Season with salt and pepper.
